In a post covid era, how you can enable contactless corporate spend management?

Manual bill submission process is no longer sustainable

Due to fear of virus transmission, activities which are done only on office premises have to be changed. Employee Expense reimburs -ements currently involve a minimum of 6 to 8 physical contacts, that can prove​ to be detrimental to the health of the employees and therefore, the organization.

End to end integrated systems – a new order of the day

End to end integrated systems (with the existing HRMS, Accounting and other IT systems) make it easier for employees and finance teams to initiate, approve, pay and post each transaction in the accounting system thereby making it seamless without involving any manual intervention

Organizations need to create a paperless world

It not only will preserve our world but also make things easier for organizations. A paperless world will also help the HR and Finance functions in building an audit trail, which will ensure total compliance and will keep the organization clean and healthy.
